A small instrument for the upstream half of work. Drop in problems
you're carrying — half-formed observations, complaints, frames in
progress — and place each one on the grid:

- **X axis:** how *clear* the problem is. Fuzzy on the left, sharp on
  the right.
- **Y axis:** how high the *stakes* are. Low at the bottom, high at
  the top.

The top-right is where you should be solving. The top-left is where you
should be framing. The bottom half is where you should be honest about
not picking it up yet.

Click anywhere on the grid to drop a card. Drag to reposition. Click a
card to edit. Your map persists in this browser; **Copy markdown** turns
it into a tidy summary you can paste anywhere.

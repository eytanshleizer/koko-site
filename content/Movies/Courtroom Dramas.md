# 🎬 Courtroom Drama Movies to Watch Together

*Curated for Eytan + girlfriend — because she loves courtroom drama.*

---

## 🏆 Must-Watch Classics

| Movie | Year | Why Watch |
|-------|------|-----------|
| **12 Angry Men** | 1957 | The ultimate jury room thriller. Tension builds in a single room. Masterclass in dialogue. |
| **To Kill a Mockingbird** | 1962 | Gregory Peck as Atticus Finch. Moral courage in the courtroom. Essential viewing. |
| **A Few Good Men** | 1992 | "You can't handle the truth!" Cruise vs. Nicholson. Military courtroom at its best. |
| **The Verdict** | 1982 | Paul Newman as a washed-up lawyer seeking redemption. Gripping and emotional. |
| **Anatomy of a Murder** | 1959 | Otto Preminger classic. Realistic trial procedure, shocking twists. |

---

## 🔥 Modern Standouts

| Movie | Year | Why Watch |
|-------|------|-----------|
| **Primal Fear** | 1996 | Richard Gere + Edward Norton. Twisted ending that will blow your mind. |
| **The Lincoln Lawyer** | 2011 | Matthew McConaughey as a slick lawyer working out of his car. Cool and clever. |
| **A Time to Kill** | 1996 | Matthew McConaughey's breakout. Racial tension, powerful closing argument. |
| **Michael Clayton** | 2007 | George Clooney. Corporate law meets thriller. Smart and tense. |
| **Fracture** | 2007 | Anthony Hopkins vs. Ryan Gosling. Cat-and-mouse legal battle. |
| **The Judge** | 2014 | Robert Downey Jr. defends his estranged father (Robert Duvall). Family drama + courtroom. |
| **Just Mercy** | 2019 | Based on true story. Michael B. Jordan fights for wrongfully convicted prisoner. Powerful. |
| **The Trial of the Chicago 7** | 2020 | Aaron Sorkin dialogue. Political trial, razor-sharp wit. Netflix. |
| **Dark Waters** | 2019 | Mark Ruffalo vs. DuPont. Environmental legal thriller. Infuriating and important. |

| Movie | Year | Why Watch |

## 📺 Series Worth Mentioning

If she loves courtroom stuff, these series are excellent:
- **The Good Wife** (2009–2016) — Legal + political + personal drama
- **Better Call Saul** (2015–2022) — Breaking Bad spinoff, incredible character study
- **Your Honor** (2020–2023) — Bryan Cranston as a judge covering up his son's crime
- **Anatomy of a Scandal** (2022) — British miniseries, courtroom + political thriller

## 🍿 Date Night Picks (In Order)

1. **Primal Fear** — Great twist, keeps you guessing
2. **A Few Good Men** — Classic, quotable, exciting
3. **The Trial of the Chicago 7** — Modern, relevant, witty
4. **Just Mercy** — Emotional, based on true events
5. **12 Angry Men** — If she loves pure courtroom tension

## ⚠️ Key Insight: The Vertical Specialization Opportunity
Firms like Ascendix (CRE) and Naitiv (insurance) are interesting because:
1. Their ontology is highly repeatable — you configure it once and every customer looks roughly the same
2. The Global Learning Layer compounds *much faster* in a single vertical
3. The product needs less configuration work to demo compellingly for them
4. They may be easier to close because the differentiation story is more concrete

**Recommendation:** Consider building one vertical-specific demo (not just CloudMasonry/Agentforce) to show the product's versatility and expand the buyer surface.

## How the Ecosystem Works

Salesforce consulting partners are companies that implement, configure, and extend Salesforce for enterprise customers. They range from 2-person boutiques to global systems integrators. The ecosystem is large (~10,000+ partners globally), stratified by tier, and currently undergoing rapid consolidation.

---

## Partner Tiers

Salesforce ranks partners in a tiered program based on certifications, customer satisfaction scores (CSAT), and revenue attributed to Salesforce.

| Tier | Description | Typical size |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| **Registered** | Entry level — minimal certs, small firms | 1–10 staff |
| **Ridge** | Growing practice, some certs, some customer proof | 10–50 staff |
| **Crest** | Established, significant certs and CSAT scores | 50–200 staff |
| **Summit** | Top tier of the standard program — high certs, high CSAT, significant Salesforce revenue | 100–500 staff |
| **Global Strategic** | Reserved for the biggest SIs (Accenture, Deloitte, IBM, Infosys, etc.) | 1,000+ SF staff |

**Note:** CloudMasonry is ranked #1 in Chicago, top 10 globally in their tier. Ascendix is a Summit partner. Plative is established with Agentforce Partner Network membership.

## The Agentforce Partner Program

Salesforce launched Agentforce in 2024/2025 as its AI-agent platform layer. Partners who specialize in Agentforce deployments have a new tier/track:

- **Agentforce Specialization** — certified expertise in building and deploying AI agents
- Partners with this specialization get preferential deal referrals from Salesforce
- It's currently a differentiator; it will become table stakes within 24 months

**Why this matters:**
- Every boutique Salesforce SI is racing to get Agentforce-certified
- The firms that arrive at the acquisition table with proven Agentforce depth command higher multiples
- The Customer Intelligence Graph is built for Agentforce practices — it maps the customer complexity that Agentforce deployments require

## The Acquisition Wave (2022–Present)

The Salesforce partner ecosystem is consolidating rapidly. Large enterprises and global SIs are buying boutique partners to acquire:
1. Salesforce certification density
2. Client relationships
3. Proven delivery methodology
4. (Increasingly) Agentforce expertise

### Notable acquisitions:

| Acquirer | Target | Size / Notes |
|----------|--------|-------------|
| **TCS** | Coastal Cloud | ~$700M — boutique Salesforce elite, US-focused |
| **Accenture** | NeuraFlash | 510 professionals, 2,000+ Salesforce certs, Agentforce specialist |
| **Cognizant** | Thirdera | ServiceNow Elite partner (relevant to Naitiv origin story) |
| **Wipro** | Capco | Financial services consulting + tech |
| **Infosys** | Various | Multiple Salesforce practices absorbed over time |

**The pattern:**
- Boutiques that can prove **thinking depth** (not just cert count) get top-dollar acquisitions
- "Thinking depth" = structured methodology, proprietary IP, demonstrated customer intelligence
- The window is approximately 12–24 months before the best targets are taken

## The Multiple Math

Salesforce partners are typically valued at **3–8x EBITDA** depending on:
- Cert density (certs per revenue dollar)
- Client concentration (is there a top-10 that would leave?)
- Proprietary IP or methodology
- CSAT and retention
- Agentforce specialization
- Geographic positioning

**What moves the multiple up:**
- Demonstrably unique methodology (not just "we do Salesforce")
- Proprietary tools or IP on the balance sheet
- High CSAT and retention (proves delivery quality)
- Agentforce specialization (currently premium-valued)

**The Customer Intelligence Graph plays here:** It's a proprietary intelligence asset that:
1. Goes on the balance sheet as IP
2. Proves methodology depth
3. Compounds over engagements
→ All three of these move the acquisition multiple

## Implications for Sales Strategy

1. **Urgency is real and finite** — the acquisition window will close. Frame every pitch with this.
2. **Proof of depth = premium multiple** — not just features, but what the product proves about the buyer
3. **Agentforce is the vector** — any firm with Agentforce ambitions is a natural buyer

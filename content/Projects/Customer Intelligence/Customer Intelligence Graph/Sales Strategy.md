## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)

**30–100 firms globally** for whom "win bigger contracts and stay relevant in the AI-agent era" is existential.

### Must-haves:
- Consulting, SI, or transformation practice
- 50–500 professionals
- Selling complex, multi-month engagements
- In an acquisition window (or actively building toward one)
- Already investing in AI/Agentforce positioning

### Best-fit signals:
- Has named practice areas (Agentforce, RPA→AI, etc.)
- Publicly highlights customer depth / methodology
- Has existing IP or productized offerings
- CEO/founder is still active and strategic

### Buyer types ranked:
1. Boutique Salesforce/Agentforce specialists (CloudMasonry, Plative)
2. Vertically specialized SIs (Ascendix/CRE, Naitiv/Insurance)
3. Mid-size generalist SIs (Accelirate, VRP)
4. Enterprise practices (Accenture, Deloitte — longer cycle, harder, but huge)

## Market Context: The Acquisition Window

This is the engine behind urgency.

### What happened:
- **TCS acquired Coastal Cloud** — ~$700M for a boutique Salesforce partner
- **Accenture acquired NeuraFlash** — 510 professionals, 2,000+ Salesforce certifications
- **Pattern:** Big SIs are buying Agentforce expertise before it gets too expensive or too rare

### The implication for buyers:
- Every independent Salesforce firm is in a **12–24 month acquisition window**
- Firms that get top-of-range multiples prove **thinking depth** — not just execution
- A proprietary intelligence asset (our product) = demonstrable proof of depth = higher exit multiple
- A firm that has *not* differentiated will be acquired at a discount — or not at all

### The pitch framing:
> "TCS paid $700M for Coastal Cloud. What made Coastal worth that? Not the headcount. Not the certs. The proof that their people think deeper than anyone else. This tool is how you demonstrate that proof — and put it on your balance sheet before the window closes."

## Deployment Model

- **Not SaaS.** Single buyer. Single strategic asset.
- Sold and configured for **one firm's** solution stack and verticals
- Learning layer is **proprietary to buyer** — compounds over their engagements only
- Optional: Eytan/team does the initial configuration (Setup Module)
- Post-sale: buyer's team runs it, OR ongoing support contract

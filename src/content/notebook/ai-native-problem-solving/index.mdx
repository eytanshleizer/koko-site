## Why this is the shape

You'll notice nothing in this system is novel on its own. There are no exotic agents. No new model. No framework you have to adopt. The point is the *composition* — five small roles, three kinds of memory, a thin orchestration loop, a clear stopping rule.

That composition is doing the work that a single LLM call cannot do, no matter how strong the model: holding the problem still long enough to be re-framed, gathering the context the model couldn't possibly know, separating "good idea" from "good idea *for this constraint*," and knowing when to stop.

The next post in this thread will be about the Conductor specifically — the part most systems get wrong, and the part that determines whether the whole thing feels like a tool or like a colleague.
